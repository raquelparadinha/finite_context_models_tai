203 The Internet is the worldwide network of all computers (or networks of computers) that communicate using a particu-lar protocol for routing data from one computer to another  .   As  long  as  the  programs  they  run  follow  the rules  of  the  protocol   the  computers  can  be  connected  by a variety of physical means including ordinary and special phone  lines   cable   fiber  optics   and  even  wireless  or  satel-lite transmission.
