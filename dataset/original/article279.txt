The  flat-panel  display  is  an  alternative  used  in  most  laptop  com-puters  and  some  higher-end  desktop  systems.   The  most common type uses a liquid crystal display (LCD).  The dis-play consists of a grid of cells with one cell for each of the three colors (red  green  and blue) for each pixel.
