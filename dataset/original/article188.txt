187 Naturally  there  must  be  software  to  manage  the  transmis-sion and reception of data packets.  The structure of a packet (sometimes  called  a frame)  has  been  standardized  with  a preamble  source and destination addresses  the data itself  a  checksum   and  two  special  layers  that  interface  with  the differing ways that Ethernet and token ring networks physi-cally handle the packets.
