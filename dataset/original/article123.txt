122 The term supply chain management was developed in the 1980s to refer to the systematic efforts to improve the efficiency and reliability of this vital business activity.  Although the details will vary with the industry  a supply chain can include the following activities:  obtaining the raw materials or components needed for the product  manufacturing finished products  marketing the product  distributing the product to retailers or other outlets  servicing the product and supporting customers  (increasingly) providing for the ultimate recycling or disposal of the product
