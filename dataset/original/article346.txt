345 Public Key (PKI) is used to verify that the signature was really generatedusing the corresponding private key.  Public keys are often registered witha third party and can be downloaded so the person can check if the key isgenuine.  (See also Digital Signatures). Public Key Infrastructure is needed as a means of generating and managing the encryption keys is required.  It is the use and management of cryptographic keys-a public key and a private key-for the secure transmission andauthentication of data across public networks.  Vendor systems do  however differ (Key Recovery  Encryption-Authorization).
