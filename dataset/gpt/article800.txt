799 eBay is an online marketplace where people can buy and sell a wide variety of goods and services.  It was founded in 1995 and has since become one of the largest e-commerce platforms in the world  with operations in many countries. On eBay  individuals and businesses can list items for sale  and buyers can browse and purchase items using a bidding or fixed-price system.  The site offers a diverse range of products  including electronics  clothing  collectibles  and much more. eBay also provides tools and services to help sellers manage their listings  process payments  and ship items to buyers.  Additionally  the site has a feedback system that allows buyers and sellers to rate and review each other  which helps establish trust and reputation within the community.
