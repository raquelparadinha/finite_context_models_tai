783 In computers  a flag is a binary value or a status indicator used to represent a condition or a specific state of a processor  a software program  or a hardware device.  Flags are used extensively in computer programming and digital circuits to control and coordinate various operations. Flags are typically represented by a single bit in a processor's register or memory location.  They can be set  cleared  or modified based on the results of arithmetic  logical  or other operations.
